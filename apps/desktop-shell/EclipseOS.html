Current design iteration

The pills tray is now a tucked ledge rather than a separate row: the gray tray peeks beneath the white input panel so the two read as one bent surface. This pass moves the tray 14px behind the panel and reflows the pills when the composer collapses.

Tray contract

  • Tuck depth: 14 px behind the input panel.
  • The tray always peeks on the bottom edge — never fully hidden.
  • Pills keep 44 px hit targets at every width.
  • Dark themes stay matte: quiet shadow only, no lit edge, no ring.

Open questions

  • Should the tray tuck animate on focus, or hold steady?
  • Does --mat-tuck belong in styles/tokens.css or stay component-local?
